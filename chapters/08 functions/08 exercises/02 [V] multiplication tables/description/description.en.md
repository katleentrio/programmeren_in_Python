Write a function `multiplication_table` that takes an integer $$n \in \mathbb{Z}$$ (`int`). The function must output the multiplication table of $$n$$ from 1 to 10.

### Example

```console?lang=python&prompt=>>>
>>> multiplication_table(12)
1 * 12 = 12
2 * 12 = 24
3 * 12 = 36
4 * 12 = 48
5 * 12 = 60
6 * 12 = 72
7 * 12 = 84
8 * 12 = 96
9 * 12 = 108
10 * 12 = 120
```
